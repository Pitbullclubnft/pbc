About

Animal friendly pet owner

Our project was created with the idea of being able to connect the NFT space with covering adoption fees for stray, abandoned, unwanted pets in need of a home. We believe in adopting animals and giving them a second chance at having a better life. By purchasing a PBC NFT, you will help giving animals a new home and creating space in animal shelters.

Collection

Collection

Pit Bull Club is a collection of 10,000 unique, digital Pitbull NFT collectibles stored as ERC-721 tokens that represent community through ownership. Owning a PBC-NFT comes with exclusive utilities reserved only for group members. The Pit Bull Club will be released on May 3, 2022. We are working to save the lives of cats and dogs all across Germany, giving pets second chances and happy homes, and that’s why we will be donating 15% of initial mint sales and 5% of after market sales to local animal shelters.
